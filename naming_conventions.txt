MQTT topics:
Devices and areas
<home>/<area>/<device_type>/<device_id>/<type>[/<value>]

<home> - home
<area> - room or area in the home
<device_type> - type of the device, e.g. light, motion, switch, plug, etc
<device_id> - if of the specific device in the area
<type> - information type, e.g. state, date_time, settings
<value> - optional value, e.g. setting name

Examples:
home/kitchen/light/ceiling/settings/hold_on
home/kitchen/light/ceiling/settings/hold_on_timeout
